CARDIFF DEVILS

The project consisted in creating all the motion graphics on the big screens for the stadium of the Cardiff devils (the hockey team of the city of Cardiff ). I also modelated the mascot with the tipical equipment of the cardiff hockey players (stick, gloves…) The company is called Lab Class. The project was done with Cinema4D and After effects.
