MAPPING

These are some frames of the motion graphics and animations I made for mappings and projections (theatre, concerts…). I was the responsible of the video-mapping content. A basic idea was given to me, and from there I had the responsibility to create the whole animated 10 minutes long video in 2 and a half weeks.

The software I worked with was Cinema 4D for the 3D content. Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects for 2D content and postproduction.
